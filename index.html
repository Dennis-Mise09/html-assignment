WORLD WIDE WEB

The World Wide Web (WWW), commonly known as the Web, is an information system enabling information to
be shared over the Internet through simplified ways meant to appeal to users beyond IT "specialists" and hobbyists,[1]
as well as documents and other web resources to be accessed over the Internet according to specific rules, the Hypertext Transfer Protocol (HTTP).
